
## Introduction
I'm a Final Fantasy gamer at heart and I wanted to blend my cybersecurity learning journey with something genuinely fun and creative. So I turned my notes and discoveries into a lore inspired project centered around the world of Final Fantasy and I'm sharing everything right here on GitHub.

I hope you enjoy following along on this adventure!

## The story begins...

One day I began to wonder what I could accomplish with the finite time I have in this world. Day after day I found myself toiling at work that left my spirit untouched. Then, while taking an introductory coding course at my local community college, I was asked to explore different fields in technology. That was when I first encountered the world of cybersecurity.

It felt like something far beyond my reach. I had no foundation in IT, some knowledge of coding, and little understanding of how computers worked beneath the surface. All I had was experience building gaming PCs and solving problems with the help of a Moogle here and there.

Yet the challenge called to me. This was a path that could grant me the skills and experience to stand against those who would bring harm through the digital world. And so, I chose to walk it.
